About Me

All my life, I have always been driven towards creativity. At an early age I discovered my passion for creating with music producing. I love the opportunity to think of a solution or feature that's "outside the box" and different, yet just effective as the normal. It extended when a friend introduced me to web development, which combined my love of technology with the ability to create.

Wanting to dive deeper, I attended Learning Fuze, an accelerated web development program, where I spent over 700 hours learning web development. I had the chance to work with a number of great developers and create many apps of my own, most notable being, Stubbies. Stubbies is an app that allows students studying the same subject, to connect with one another, creating events that allow other users to find and join.

I look forward to working in an environment that would allow my creativity to grow. Please call or email to discuss working together.
